Cookies Policy

Last updated October 30th, 2023

We only use Strictly Necessary Cookies on our websites. These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in order to show contact forms, to facilitate secure area access, and for allowing shopping cart functionality.

You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies if you'd prefer, but then some parts of the site will not work.

Strictly Necessary Cookies are exempt from the consent requirements of privacy laws, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive (also known as the Cookie Law) in the European Union, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) in California.

We don't use cookies to improve the performance of our website or to track visitors. We use services like Plausible to collect visitor statistics, but this service doesn't use cookies to track your repeat visits or collect your personal information.

When it comes to social media, we use non-tracking links and serve icon badge images from our own server. For YouTube videos, we use the 'no-cookie' URL in order to play videos without your identity being collected. YouTube videos only become embedded on our website, after you have chosen to play the video. Again further preventing tracking.

Why do we do this? Because having a fast and responsive website - without pop-up consent boxes that interrupt navigation flow - helps us to provide a better user experience for our visitors, which is core to our philosophy when it comes to providing our regulatory products and services.

If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to cookies, please contact us.

Please see our Privacy Policy for further information about how we manage your data.
